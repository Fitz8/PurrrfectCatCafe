About our purrrfect cafe...

We are a family run cafe, combining both our love for coffee and our love for cats to create the purrrfect dining experience. Our specialities are home-made cakes, a diverse selection of cozy hot drinks and tasty classic lunch options, all lovingly prepared.

It is important to us that all of our adopted cats are from rescue centres and are well looked after. We provide them with grooming, high quality cat food, play areas, scratching posts and of course, lots of love.

In the future, we plan to engage in many charity fundraising events, to make sure that as many cats as possible find stable, suitable homes. Please keep an eye on our social media pages to keep up with updates on our cats, events and future projects.
